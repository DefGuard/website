Defguard is free and open source with most functionalities. Also all enterprise features are free up to the limit of:

- 5 users
- 10 devices
- 1 location

More details about enterprise functionalities can be found here: https://docs.defguard.net/enterprise/all-enteprise-features. Below you can buy a license (https://docs.defguard.net/enterprise/license) to enable enterprise features on your instance.
